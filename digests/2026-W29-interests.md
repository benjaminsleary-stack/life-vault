# Weekly Interests — 2026-W29

First run of this scout — a couple of items are a few weeks old but genuinely new to surface.

## Music

- **Boards of Canada — "Inferno"** (Warp Records, released 29 May 2026). Their first studio album since *Tomorrow's Harvest* (2013) — ambient/hauntology with biblical/occult undertones, closer to your darkwave/ambient lane than anything else in their catalogue. [Album on Bandcamp](https://boardsofcanada.bandcamp.com/album/inferno) · [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inferno_(Boards_of_Canada_album))
