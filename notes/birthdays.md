## What to know

Birthdays pulled from the personal calendar for people who do not (yet) have their
own note. Kept here rather than as fifteen thin person notes — a date alone is not
a reason to create an entity. If someone starts coming up regularly, promote them
to `people/` and move the date with them.

People with their own notes carry their own `(occasion::)` line and are not
duplicated here.
